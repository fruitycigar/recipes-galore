Orecchiette Pasta with Turnip Greens

INGREDIENTS

  • 1 lb of fresh or dry orecchiette (450g)d
  • 1/2 lb of turnip greens (leaves and floret) (225g)
  • 50 gr of breadcrumbs
  • 1 whole red chili pepper, either Fresno or Anaheim
  • 1 clove of garlic
  • 3 salt- or oil-packed anchovy fillets, or 2 tsp of a black olive paste (made from Ligurian olives would be the best one)
  • Coarse and fine salt
  • 3 tbsp of extra virgin olive oil

METHOD

1.Clean the turnip greens: discard the external larger leaves and the stalks. With the help of a sharp knife, remove the soft leaves and the florets;

2.Properly rinse them under running water, dry them, and put them aside;

3.Fill a large stockpot with plenty of salted water and bring to a boil;

4.When the water boils, add the turnip greens and cook for 5 minutes;

5.Toast the breadcrumbs until golden over medium heat in a pan with 1 tablespoon of olive oil;

6.Rinse the anchovies under running water to wash off the salt and oil;

7.In another pan heat the remaining oil, add the red pepper flakes, the unpeeled garlic clove, and the anchovy fillets. Sauté over medium-low heat; otherwise; the high temperature will make the oil and the anchovies bitter. 8.Simmer it very very slowly;

9.Stir with a wooden spoon until the anchovy fillets have melted; Cook for a few minutes, remove from the heat, and discard the garlic. If you are vegan, you can add black olive paste as a substitute for the anchovy;

10.After the turnip greens have cooked for 5 minutes, add the orecchiette and follow the cooking time on the package; stir gently;

11.When done, drain orecchiette with the turnip greens; remember to save a scoop of cooking water: you may need it in case the sauce gets too thick. Pour them into the pan with the anchovy sauce;

12.Sauté gently and season with salt;

13.Taste it and see if it needs some more oil;

14.Remove it from the heat, place the orecchiette in bowls, and top with a drizzle of olive oil and the toasted breadcrumbs. This is a special dish that you must share with your family;

Buon appetito!
